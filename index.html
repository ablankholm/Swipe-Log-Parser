Purpose

The predefined CDVI swipe-in-swipe-out reports are ill suited for automation, analysis and immediate comparison in Excel. This is an F# script that parses the CSV output of said CDVI reports and reconstructs the data in a more accessible tabular format.

Requirements

  • CDVI reporting software installed on your system.
